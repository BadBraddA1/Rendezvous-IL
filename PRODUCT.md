# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** Prospective homeschool families researching Rendezvous 2027 for the first time.

They are often parents planning a multi-day retreat with kids of mixed ages. They browse on phones between school and errands, compare dates and lodging, and look for signals of community, faith, and whether the event feels organized and welcoming—not corporate or gimmicky.

**Secondary (out of scope for default design register):** Returning families, registered attendees (account tools), and event admins. Admin surfaces follow product-register patterns when worked on explicitly.

## Product Purpose

Rendezvous IL is the public home for a Christian homeschool family retreat at Lake Williamson Christian Center (Carlinville, IL). The site should help prospective families understand what Rendezvous is, when registration opens (January 1, 2027 for the May 3–7, 2027 event), what to expect (schedule, lodging, meals, Bible Bowl theme), and why the community is worth joining.

Success looks like: families feel confident enough to return when registration opens, share the site with other homeschool families, and trust the Bradds / organizing team behind the event.

## Brand Personality

**Joyful · Family-rooted · Unhurried**

Voice is warm and direct—not salesy, not childish. Faith is present but not preachy. The site should feel like a trusted camp you've heard about from friends: established, outdoorsy, human.

## Anti-references

- **Overly cartoonish kids-camp branding** — loud novelty fonts, clip-art energy, rainbow chaos
- Generic SaaS landing pages — gradient metric heroes, glass blur, startup card grids
- Stock church website templates — busy sidebars, dated sliders, cluttered navigation
- Corporate conference sites — cold, transactional, hotel-retreat polish

## Design Principles

1. **Invite without hype** — Help families imagine the week; don't pressure or oversell.
2. **Clarity for busy parents** — Scannable dates, lodging, and "what happens" on small screens first.
3. **Grounded in place** — Lake Williamson, Illinois outdoors, real photography over abstract decoration.
4. **Faithful and family-centered** — Community and scripture (e.g. 2027 theme: 1 Samuel) without church-template clutter.
5. **Accessible by default** — Multi-generational families; readable type, motion that respects preferences, strong contrast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **Target:** WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, with extra care for older users and parents managing the site one-handed on phones.
- **Motion:** Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`; never hide essential content behind entrance animations.
- **Contrast:** Body and muted text must stay readable on tinted backgrounds—not washed-out gray for "elegance."
- **Touch:** Primary actions and navigation affordances ≥ 44×44px where possible.
